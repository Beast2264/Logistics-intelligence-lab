# Supply Chain Network Model

The Logistics Intelligence Lab simulates a simplified supply chain visibility system.

Entities modeled include:

Suppliers  
Facilities / Distribution Centers  
Shipments  
Trucks  
Drivers  
Routes  

Purpose

The goal is to model how shipments move through a transportation network and how fleet telemetry and dispatch systems interact with supply chain operations.

This allows experimentation with:

• shipment visibility  
• fleet monitoring  
• logistics analytics  
• AI-assisted decision support
